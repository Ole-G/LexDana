Our Mission

Making U.S. Intellectual Property Data Accessible and Intelligent

LexDana exists because legal research shouldn't require specialized database training. IP professionals need answers to strategic questions, not more dashboards to learn or filters to configure.

We believe data should answer questions, not create more of them. Our mission is to make comprehensive IP intelligence accessible through natural conversation—the way attorneys actually think and work.

What We Do

Conversational Access to 15+ Years of U.S. IP Data

LexDana combines verified IP datasets with advanced language models to help professionals explore the litigation and prosecution landscape with unprecedented speed and ease.

We built a natural language interface to structured legal data—PTAB proceedings, district court cases, ITC investigations, and USPTO filings—that understands questions the way you'd ask a research analyst. No SQL queries, no Boolean operators, no manual spreadsheet building.

Ask about judge tendencies, law firm track records, petitioner strategies, or examiner patterns. Get structured results with charts, tables, and direct citations to source documents. Export for client communications or court filings. That's it.

Our Philosophy

Speed Meets Precision in Legal Research

Legal research has two competing demands: it needs to be fast enough to support litigation timelines, and accurate enough to stake your professional reputation on. Traditional platforms make you choose between speed and depth.

LexDana delivers both by handling the time-consuming data gathering so you can focus on analysis and strategy. We don't replace attorney judgment—we accelerate the research foundation that judgment depends on.

Every result includes source citations you can verify independently. When we calculate statistics, we show sample sizes so you can assess confidence. We refresh data every 48 hours from official sources. This transparency isn't optional—it's required when IP attorneys work under Rule 11 sanctions.

Our Values

Precision

Every data point traces to verified public records from USPTO, PTAB, and federal courts. We organize and connect existing data—we don't generate or infer information.

Transparency

Source citations, data freshness indicators, and sample size disclosure on every search result. You can verify everything we show you.

Efficiency

Research that normally takes hours should take minutes. Time saved on data gathering means more time for strategic thinking and client service.
